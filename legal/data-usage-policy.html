Data Usage Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026 · Version 1.6

1. Purpose

This Data Usage Policy provides a detailed guide to how your conversation data is collected, processed, stored, and shared within the Closer AI application. This policy supplements our Privacy Policy and is designed to give you full transparency into our data flows.

2. Audio Recording Data Flow

When you record a conversation, the following process occurs:

2.1 Capture

Audio is captured on your device using your device's microphone. Before any recording can begin, both partners must each enter their own private consent PIN on the device (dual consent). Recording requires a linked partner — there is no solo or single-person recording mode — so every recording carries the active, on-device consent of both partners. It remains your responsibility to ensure all parties consent as required by the recording laws in your jurisdiction.

2.2 Upload & Storage

The recorded audio file is uploaded to Supabase Storage using an encrypted connection (TLS). Audio files are stored with encryption at rest and are protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring only you and your linked partner (if applicable) can access them.

2.3 Transcription

A Supabase Edge Function sends the audio recording to Deepgram for speech-to-text transcription. The resulting transcript text is stored in the database, associated with your recording. Deepgram processes the audio solely to produce the transcript, and we send the request with model-training opt-out enabled so your audio is not used to train Deepgram's models.

2.4 Post-Transcription Audio Storage & Retention

When a recording is created, its audio receives a fixed deletion date 90 days later. Existing recordings already assigned a 7-day or 30-day deadline keep that shorter date. Before the deadline, both original participants may approve one additional 180 days; the extension cannot renew or stack, and either participant may end it. The full transcript is stored separately and receives its own fixed 12-month deletion date. Audio files are never sent to AI analysis providers — only transcript text is used for AI analysis.

3. AI Analysis Data Flow

After transcription, your conversation is analyzed by AI:

3.1 What Is Sent to AI

Only the conversation transcript (text) is sent to the AI language model provider for analysis. Your original audio recording is NOT sent to the AI provider.

3.2 What AI Generates

The AI produces a conversation summary highlighting key topics and themes, relationship insights based on communication patterns, and personalized coaching suggestions. These generated outputs are stored in the database and associated with your recording.

3.3 AI Data Handling

Our AI provider, Anthropic (Claude AI), processes your transcript solely to generate the requested analysis. Anthropic does not use this data to train its models, and retains API inputs and outputs for up to 30 days solely for safety and security purposes, after which they are deleted.

4. Partner Data Sharing Model

Closer AI supports partner account linking for shared access to relationship data:

4.1 Partner Linking

Partners are linked by email invitation. When both partners have created accounts and their email addresses match the invitation records, accounts are automatically linked.

4.2 Automatic Summary Sharing

When accounts are linked, AI-generated conversation summaries are automatically visible to both partners. This allows both partners to benefit from insights without requiring full transcript access.

4.3 Transcript Mutual Approval

Full conversation transcripts are only shared when both partners approve. Either partner may decline to share a transcript, and that decision is respected. This ensures both partners have control over the level of detail shared.

5. Consent Mechanisms

Closer AI implements multiple layers of consent to ensure you maintain control over your data:

5.1 Onboarding Consent

During onboarding, you are asked to acknowledge three consent checkboxes: consent to conversation analysis and AI processing, consent that both parties agree to recording and analysis, and agreement to the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Data Usage Policy.

5.2 Per-Recording Consent

Before each recording begins, both partners must each enter their own private consent PIN on the same device. Recording requires a linked partner and is not available solo, so every conversation captured carries the active, on-device consent of both partners. You remain responsible for complying with the recording-consent laws in your jurisdiction.

5.3 Transcript Mutual Approval

Sharing full transcripts between partners requires explicit approval from both parties on a per-transcript basis.

5.4 Audio Retention Extension

Either participant may request one additional 180 days for a specific audio recording. The extension takes effect only if both original participants approve before the original deadline. It cannot renew or stack, and a pending request does not pause deletion. Either participant may later withdraw their own approval, returning the audio to its original schedule; if that date has passed, the cleanup service removes it.

6. What We Do NOT Do

To be clear about our data practices, we want to explicitly state what we do NOT do: we do NOT sell your data to any third party; we do NOT use your conversations to train AI models; we do NOT share identifiable conversation content with anyone other than your linked partner (with appropriate consent); we do NOT access your data without proper authentication and authorization; we do NOT use your conversation content for advertising or marketing purposes; and we do NOT share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

7. Data Minimization

We follow the principle of data minimization. We only collect information that is necessary to provide the service. Audio recordings are only created when you actively initiate a recording with consent. Audio is time-limited, and full transcripts are generated solely to enable AI analysis and your review before their 12-month deletion date. When the transcript is deleted, source-heavy intermediate analysis is also removed and the retained insight history is reduced to summaries, patterns, and no more than ten short excerpts. Account information collected during onboarding is limited to what is needed for the service to function.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

8.1 Automatic Retention Policy

Raw audio is automatically deleted 90 days after recording unless both participants approve the one permitted 180-day extension. Existing recordings already assigned a 7-day or 30-day deadline keep that shorter date. Full transcripts are automatically deleted after 12 months. Transcript deletion also removes speaker samples and source-heavy intermediate analysis; summaries, patterns, and no more than ten short transcript excerpts remain while the relationship history is active. Either participant may delete sooner. Conversations and retained history are also deleted through full deletion, unlinking, account deletion, or after both participants have been inactive for 24 months. Advance content-free notices are sent before scheduled audio and transcript deletion. A narrow, expiring legal hold may temporarily delay automatic deletion when legally required.

8.2 Individual Recording Deletion

You may remove available components from the Conversations screen. Audio-only deletion is immediate for the recording owner and leaves the transcript and analysis. Transcript-only deletion removes the verbatim transcript, transcript-derived speaker samples, legacy analysis, and source-heavy intermediate pipeline data for both participants. The retained insight snapshot may still contain summaries, patterns, and up to ten short excerpts. Either partner may unilaterally schedule full deletion; no approval from the other is required. Full deletion uses a 7-day undo window and then permanently removes the audio, transcript, summaries, insights, short excerpts, and consent evidence, subject only to a documented legal obligation.

8.3 Account Deletion

Deleting your account removes all of your data, including your profile, AI chat history, recordings, transcripts, summaries, and insights. If you are linked to a partner, deletion also unlinks the relationship and withdraws consent to retain shared recordings as described below: every recording attributed to the two of you is permanently deleted, regardless of which partner started it. Your partner's account and recordings not attributed to your relationship are not deleted. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

8.4 Partner Unlinking

Keeping shared recordings requires both partners' consent. When either partner unlinks, that consent is withdrawn: all recordings the two of you made together — including audio, transcripts, and AI-generated analyses — are permanently deleted from both accounts. This deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. If you both want to keep a copy, use the partner-approved data export in Settings before unlinking. Unlinking also revokes all ongoing access to each other's data.

9. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data: all data is transmitted using TLS (Transport Layer Security) encryption; stored data, including audio files and transcripts, is encrypted at rest; Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data and authorized shared data; authentication is required for all data operations; our service providers maintain their own security certifications and practices; and we conduct regular security reviews and monitoring.

10. Third-Party Data Processing

Our third-party service providers process your data under their standard terms and applicable data-protection obligations. Where required, we enter into Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with our providers, and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards are relied upon for international data transfers where applicable. Each provider is obligated to process your data only as needed to perform its service and to maintain appropriate security measures. We review our providers' security practices and compliance.

11. Contact

For questions about how your data is used, please contact us at data@getcloserai.com.
