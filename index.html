Private & secure by design

Relationships require trust — and so does the tool you use to strengthen them.

🤝

Dual consent required

Both partners must explicitly approve before any recording can be made. One-sided recording is never allowed — Closer is built for mutual growth, not surveillance.

🔐

Controlled access

Transcripts and analyses are private by default. Each partner independently controls whether to share — nothing is visible to the other without explicit permission.

🛡️

Encrypted & secure

Your recordings and personal data are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data or use it to train AI models.

🗑️

You own your data

Delete any recording, transcript, or your entire account at any time. Limited records may be retained where law requires — see our Privacy Policy.
